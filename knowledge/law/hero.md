## Hero 标题公式
```
[执业领域] solicitors in [城市] — [差异化一句话]
```
### 实战例子（UK 本地）
- "Family Law Solicitors in Leeds"
- "Immigration Solicitors in Manchester"
- "Commercial Property Lawyers in Bristol"
- "Wills & Probate Solicitors in <area>"

### 反模式
- "Best solicitors in town"（空话 + SRA 合规敏感，避免绝对化表述）
- emoji 堆砌（⚖️ 与反 emoji 立场冲突）

## Hero 副标题套路
- 信任： "Regulated by the SRA · fixed-fee initial consultation"
- 领域： "Specialists in <practice area> for individuals and businesses"
- 在地： "Serving <city> and <region> since <year>"

## 品牌写法（About / Story）
- 锚定： "<Name> is a <practice> law firm based on <street>, <city>."
- 信任： "Regulated by the SRA (SRA No. <n>); <years> years helping <clients>."
- 理由： "Clear fees, plain-English advice, and a dedicated solicitor."

## 差异化角度库（选 1–2）
- 费用： "Fixed-fee · No-obligation first call"
- 领域： "Specialist <niche> team"
- 服务： "Remote & in-person · evenings by appointment"
- 信任： "SRA regulated · <awards/accreditations>"

## 合规红线（UK）
- 不得暗示结果保证（"we win 99% cases" 违规）
- 必须标明 SRA 监管与注册号
- 用词正式、Plain English（反法律黑话）
