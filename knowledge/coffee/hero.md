## Hero 标题公式
```
[定位] coffee in [城市/街区] — [差异化一句话]
```
### 实战例子（UK 本地）
- "Speciality Coffee in Leeds"
- "Third-Wave Coffee Roastery in Bristol"
- "Independent Café on <street>, <city>"
- "Pour-over & Pastry in Manchester"

### 反模式
- "Best coffee in town"（空话）
- emoji 堆砌（☕ 与反 emoji 立场冲突）

## Hero 副标题套路
- 工艺： "Single-origin espresso · in-house roasting · batch brew"
- 场景： "A calm corner for slow mornings and laptop afternoons"
- 供应链： "Beans from <roastery/region>, brewed with care"

## 品牌写法（About / Story）
- 锚定： "<Name> is an independent speciality café on <street>, <city>."
- 人格： "Founded by <owner>, we roast / brew <philosophy>."
- 理由： "Expect <signature drink> and a short food menu."

## 差异化角度库（选 1–2）
- 烘焙： "In-house roastery · weekly single origins"
- 工艺： "Pour-over bar · espresso only / full menu"
- 场景： "Work-friendly · Plant-based · Dog-friendly"
- 社区： "Local hub · events / workshops"
