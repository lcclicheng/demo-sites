## 通用模板
1. **Do you roast your own beans?** — <Yes, on-site / Yes, in small batches / No, we source from <roastery>>.
2. **Are you dog-friendly?** — <Yes, well-behaved dogs welcome / Outdoor only>.
3. **Is there wifi / can I work here?** — Free wifi; <laptop-friendly all day / busy mornings, quieter afternoons>.
4. **Do you have plant-based options?** — Oat / soy / almond milk as standard; vegan cakes daily.
5. **What are your opening hours?** — <hours>; kitchen/closing <time>.
6. **Do you do takeaway?** — Yes, bring a reusable cup for <discount> off.
7. **Is there parking?** — <nearest car park> <distance>; limited street parking.

## 写作规则
