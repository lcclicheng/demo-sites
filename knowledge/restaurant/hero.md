## Hero 标题公式

```
[菜系] in [城市/街区] — [差异化一句话]
```

### 实战例子（UK 本地）
- "Authentic Neapolitan Pizza in York"
- "Modern British Dining in Leeds"
- "Sourdough & Small Plates in Bristol"
- "Family Italian Restaurant in Manchester"

### 反模式（勿用）
- "Best restaurant in town"（空话，Google 不买账，且显网红）
- emoji 堆砌（🍕🔥✨ 与全站反 emoji 立场冲突）
- 夸大 superlative 无依据

## Hero 副标题套路（强调差异点，2 句内）
- 食材/工艺： "Slow-fermented dough · 48-hour brisket · open fire"
- 氛围/场景： "A relaxed neighbourhood spot for weeknight dinners and weekend long lunches"
- 来源/信任： "From the team behind < sister venue >"

## 品牌写法（About / Story）
- 第一句锚定地点与品类："<Name> is a <cuisine> restaurant on <street>, <city>."
- 第二句给人格："Run by <chef/owner>, we cook <philosophy>."
- 第三句给行动理由："Expect <signature dish> and a short, seasonal menu."

## 差异化角度库（按客户实际选 1–2 个）
- 产区/供应链： "Cumbrian beef · Cornish fish"
- 工艺： "Wood-fired · 48-hour ferment · house-made"
- 场景： "Date night · Family Sunday · Private hire"
- 价格带： "Weekday set lunch £ / No surprises"
