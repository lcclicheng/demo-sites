## Hero 标题公式
```
[Hair & Beauty] Salon in [城市/街区] — [差异化一句话]
```

### 实战例子（UK 本地）
- "Editorial Hair Salon in Bristol"
- "Curly Hair Specialists in Leeds"
- "Colour & Cut Studio in Manchester"
- "Men's Grooming & Beard in York"

### 反模式（勿用）
- "Best salon in town"（空话，显网红）
- emoji 堆砌（💇✨ 与全站反 emoji 立场冲突）
- "Beauty for everyone" 无差异泛词

## Hero 副标题套路（强调专长/场景，2 句内）
- 专长： "Precision cutting · conscious colour · curly specialists"
- 场景： "A calm studio for a proper consultation and a slow coffee"
- 信任： "Named one of <local press> 'best new salons'"

## 品牌写法（About / Story）
- 第一句锚定地点与品类："<Name> is a <hair & beauty salon> on <street>, <city>."
- 第二句给人格："Founded by <owner/stylist>, we focus on <philosophy>."
- 第三句给行动理由："Expect a proper consultation and <signature service>."

## 差异化角度库（按客户实际选 1–2 个）
- 专长： "Curly / textured hair · Blonde specialists · Bridal"
- 理念： "Low-tox colour · Sustainable salon · Training academy"
- 场景： "Wedding & events · Men's grooming · Kids' cuts"
- 体验： "Consultation-first · Relaxing space · Walk-ins welcome"
