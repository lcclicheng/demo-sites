## 通用模板（按客户实际删改，勿全放）
1. **What time is check-in / check-out?** — From <time>; check-out by <time>.
2. **Is parking available?** — Yes, <free/on-site> for guests; EV charge <available>.
3. **Are dogs / children welcome?** — <Dog-friendly rooms / Family rooms>; see <link>.
4. **Do you serve breakfast?** — Yes, <times>; <options>.
5. **Can I cancel / amend my booking?** — <policy>; via <link/contact>.
6. **Is there Wi-Fi?** — Yes, free throughout.
7. **How do I get there?** — <nearest station/road>; <walk> from <landmark>.
8. **Do you have accessible rooms?** — Yes, <details>; ask at booking.

## 写作规则
- 每答 ≤ 2 句，直接答、不绕弯
- 含真实电话/地址/链接（从 examples JSON 取）
- 与 seo.md 的长尾词自然重合（如 "dog friendly hotel <area>"）
- 假数据（演示站）用占位并标 unverified，真客户替换后去标
